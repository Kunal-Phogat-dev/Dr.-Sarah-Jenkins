Meet Dr. Sarah Jenkins

Chief Dermatologist & Founder

Dr. Sarah Jenkins graduated with honors from King's College London and completed her dermatology residency at Johns Hopkins University. She believes in treating the root cause of skin conditions rather than just the symptoms, taking a holistic approach to patient well-being and long-term skin health.

She is fluent in English, Arabic, and French, ensuring clear and comfortable communication with our diverse international patient base.

  • Fellowship in Aesthetic Dermatology from National Skin Centre, Singapore
  • Fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology (FAAD)

Specialized Treatments

Comprehensive care for all your dermatological needs.

Clinical Dermatology

Expert diagnosis and treatment for acne, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, and other chronic skin conditions.

Aesthetic Procedures

Safe, effective anti-aging treatments including Botox, dermal fillers, chemical peels, and laser resurfacing.

Hair & Scalp Care

Advanced solutions for hair loss, dandruff, and scalp infections, including PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy.
